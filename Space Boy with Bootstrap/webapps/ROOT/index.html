Biography

Space Boy was born in the distant year of 4580. Humanity has colonized all of the habitable planets in the Milky Way Galaxy, and space travel is incredibly advanced. At a young age, Space Boy set out on his own and accidentally got sucked into a wormhole! He woke up in our present time. Space Boy discovered the only planet with humans at the time, Earth, and has made a life for himself there helping his ancestors with their problems.

While Space Boy can't speak the ancient dialect of these early Earthlings, his suit is equipped with a universal translator, so he can at least understand them. He wears his suit everywhere he goes in order to understand what people are saying, which turns more than a few heads. Space Boy travels the whole planet helping people, but has taken a particular liking to Cityville, so that's where he spends most of his time. He uses his high-tech gadgets to help make our lives easier! From teleportation to holograms to what our primitive eyes may consider to be magic; nothing is impossible with Space Boy!
